"Patria Grande" is a term often used to refer to the concept of a united and integrated Latin America, emphasizing solidarity, cooperation, and shared cultural heritage among the countries of the region. This vision promotes the idea of a unified Latin American identity that transcends national borders and fosters collaboration for the collective well-being of its people.
